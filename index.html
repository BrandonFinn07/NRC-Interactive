Creative Media

This course is aimed at sudents who want to specialise in Interactive (Digital) Media Production from an early stage. We introduce you to a range of skills, including :

  • -Animation
  • -Website Production
  • -Graphic Design
  • -Digital Photography

There is a growing demand for Digital Media skills throughout all sectors of the economy; every business needs a website and the animation industry is thriving in the Belfast area. This entry-level course will set you on a very definite pathway to employment. Potentially, you can develop your Interactive Media skills at the Newtownabbey campus all the way up to Level 5 HND if you want, where you will work in partnership with industry.
It aims to allow learners to:

  • -Develop advanced photography knowledge and skills
  • -Learn the basics of CSS and HTML
  • -Get to know industry level software

Animation

Animation is a rapidly-expanding sector in Ireland North and South, with companies selling home-grown programmes all over the world in TV, Film and Games. We are offering students an exciting new course, where they will specialise in Story development, 2D and 3D Animation Production, Character Creation, 3D Modelling, Illustration and Digital Graphics, Visual Effects and Stop Motion animation.
Students will have the opportunity to study:

  • -Character and Environment Design
  • -How to create stories for animation
  • -2D animation
  • -3D modelling and animation
  • -Stop motion animation

About Us

Computer Labs

NRC offer a range of computer labs / MAC rooms that all students have the ability to use for their project. Each student are provided with their own log in to keep work secure. These pieces of tech are all top of the range and are all loaded with professional, company used software. If needed, students are able to loan out equipment which allow students to work on projects at home .

Adobe

When students are registered and join the course, they will have access to the full Adobe Suite. Company used software such as Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Premiere and Lightroom. The College prides itself on this as not many colleges within Northern Ireland offer this type of software that students are also able to use on their personal computers outside of College. The college also provide Adobe certificates for students. This benifits students as it help them stand our from others in the field of work as this type of training can be expensive.

Equipment

The College also provides students with quality equipment such as cameras, microphones, tripods and other equipments to carry out indivigual and group projects. Other equipment such as reading/research material can be found at the library. The library service has been designed to support your learning both on and off campus.
